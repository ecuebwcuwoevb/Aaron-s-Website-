About Me

Hi! I'm Aaron. I live in Ireland and I’m currently studying Law. I also study Psychology as an elective subject and plan to sit the FE1 exams to become a solicitor.

I speak English and conversational Irish, though I hope to become fluent. I’m also learning German as I plan to continue my studies in Germany.

I’m passionate about music — I’m a Grade 5 pianist and enjoy midwest emo and indie rock. I also play video games, mostly Pokémon, though I’m always open to recommendations!

I love reading philosophy, classics, and psychology. My favourite authors include Chekhov, Nietzsche, and Christopher Bollas. I also enjoy discussing ideas and debates of all kinds.

Most of what I watch is true crime — documentaries, investigations, and interrogations — but I’m also a big fan of anime and manga like Death Note, Tokyo Ghoul, and Blood on the Tracks.

Lately, I’ve been learning about technology and exploring the basics of coding and computing.
